# Legal templates moved

The company-level legal **templates** (Pilot NDA, Privacy Policy + DPA, Platform
User Agreement, Platform Use & Disclaimer) now live in one canonical home:

→ **`_legal-docs/`** (see `_legal-docs/README.md`)

This avoids keeping two editable copies that drift apart. When onboarding a new
agency, pull the current template from `_legal-docs/`, then file the **executed,
agency-specific copy** under `Agency Onboarding/<DEPT>/` (as with EGPD's signed
service agreement here). Blank templates → `_legal-docs/`; signed agency copies →
the agency folder.
