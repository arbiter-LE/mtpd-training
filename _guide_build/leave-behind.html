What Chiefs Ask Us
Q.Is the content actually relevant to my department?
Yes — that's the core of how Arbiter LE works. Your modules are built around your own general orders and SOPs, used word for word, never reworded or generalized. Scenarios use your jurisdiction's real roads. Any court case referenced is verified against the actual ruling. Your officers train on your policies and your reality.
Q.What happens to my officers' data — and who can see it?
Your department gets its own private, isolated database. Your data never mixes with another agency's. Each officer sees only their own record; command staff sees only their department. Officer training records are never exported, shared, or used outside your agency.
Q.How do I know my officers actually completed the training?
Every module pairs decision-based scenarios — not passive click-through — with a knowledge check of at least eight questions, each answerable directly from that module's material. The platform tracks completion, scores, and attempts. You get a documented record of engagement with your own policies.
Q.What if something goes wrong on the platform?
You contact us directly and it gets resolved quickly — typically the same day. Support comes straight from the person who built it. No ticket queues, no call-center runaround.
Q.Who built this?
Arbiter LE was built by a working law enforcement officer with more than a decade on the job — someone who has driven the roads, worked under the policies, and sat through the same training your officers do. You work directly with the person who builds and stands behind the product.
