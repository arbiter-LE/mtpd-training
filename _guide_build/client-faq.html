What Departments Ask Us

Arbiter LE is a scenario-based training platform built around your department's own policies and your own jurisdiction. Here are the questions we hear most often.

Built by a working law enforcement officer and proven in a live pilot with a Pennsylvania police department. The platform is in active daily use by sworn officers and command staff.
Q.Is the training content actually relevant to my department?
Yes — that's the core of how Arbiter LE works. Your modules are built around your department's own general orders and SOPs, used word for word, never reworded or generalized. Scenarios use your jurisdiction's real roads and conditions. Any court case referenced is verified against the actual ruling. Your officers train on your policies and your reality — not generic national content recycled across hundreds of agencies.
Q.Who's behind the platform?
Arbiter LE was built by a working law enforcement officer with more than a decade on the job — someone who has driven the roads, worked under the policies, and sat through the same training your officers do. That perspective is built into every module. You're working directly with the person who builds and stands behind the product, not a sales channel.
Q.What happens to my officers' data, and who can see it?
Your department gets its own private, isolated database. Your data never mixes with another agency's. Each officer sees only his or her own record; you and your command staff see only your department. Officer training records are never exported, shared, or used to train any outside system or AI. Your policies and your data stay yours, completely walled off from every other agency.
Q.How do I know my officers actually completed the training?
Every module pairs decision-based scenarios — not passive click-through — with a knowledge check of at least eight questions, each answerable directly from that module's material. The platform tracks completion, scores, and attempts. You get a real, documented record of engagement with your own policies, not a checkbox.
Q.How does this fit with my existing policies and training?
Arbiter LE reinforces your existing policy and verified law — it never replaces your judgment, your command decisions, or your department's orders, and it never invents policy. It is a tool to help your officers engage with and retain the policies you already have in place, documented module by module.
Q.What if something goes wrong on the platform?
You contact us directly and it gets resolved quickly — typically the same day. The platform runs on stable, automated infrastructure, and support comes straight from the person who built it. No ticket queues, no call-center runaround.
Q.How does pricing work?
Simple and transparent: a flat annual rate based on your department's size, billed annually — no setup fees, no free trials, no surprises. Reach out and we'll put clear numbers in front of you.
